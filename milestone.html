Due: April 20, 2025

Accomplishments to Date

We have set up and verified the baseline MAtCha Gaussian rendering pipeline on our local machines, and begun exploring integration points with NeuManifold’s differentiable marching cubes.

Preliminary Results

Initial renderings demonstrate correct chart‑based shading; geometry extracted via DiffMC shows a coarse mesh that we plan to refine with mesh‑aware loss.

Reflection on Progress

Our Week 1 goals were met, but integrating backprop through the mesh proved trickier than expected. We spent extra time debugging gradient propagation through the Marching Cubes implementation.

Updated Work Plan

  • Week 4 (Apr 28–May 5): Optimize joint pipeline, finalize visuals, and prepare final presentation.
